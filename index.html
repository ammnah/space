Saudi Arabia in Space & the Journey of Rayyanah Barnawi and Ali AlQarni

Introduction

Saudi Arabia has made significant progress in space exploration, launching several initiatives to support scientific research and innovation in this field.

The Journey of Rayyanah Barnawi & Ali AlQarni

In 2023, Saudi astronauts Rayyanah Barnawi, the first Saudi female astronaut, and Ali AlQarni embarked on a historic mission as part of Axiom Mission 2 to the International Space Station (ISS).

Saudi Arabia’s Achievements in Space

  • Launched the first Saudi satellite in 2000.
  • Sent astronauts on Axiom Mission 2 in 2023.
  • Established the Saudi Space Commission in 2018.
  • Collaborated with international space agencies on various research projects.
